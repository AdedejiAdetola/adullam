About The Adullam Retreat

The Adullam Retreat is an annual camping program of believers with genuine hunger and passionate love for God where we gather to wait on the lord in extended hours of prayers and stretched hours of teaching of the word of God, with several manifestations of the spirit of God. This is the maiden edition of this retreat, the lord has called for it and He will be glorified.

Stretched hours of prayer

Stretched hours of teaching

12 hours of praying in the holy ghost

Preaching the Gospel and Outreach

Breakout Sessions

Corporate Meditations

3-Days Marathon Fasting
